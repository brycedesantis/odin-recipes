Baguette Pizza

Description

A fun twist on the classic pizza we all know and love. The use of a baguette allows a family to each get their own individual pizza. A great way to teach young kids how to cook by giving them a chance to make their own pizza at home. It's simple enough that it can be made with as many toppings as desired. It can even be used to make healthy options for those with dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce, or as needed
  • 2/3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 ounces cooked and crumbled sausage
  • 2 ounces mini pepperoni slices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Split baguette in half lengthwise. Warm pizza sauce in a microwave-safe bowl until hot, about 45 seconds.
  3. Spread pizza sauce on baguette, sprinkle on cheese, and scatter sausage and pepperoni on top as desired. Place pizza on a baking tray.
  4. Bake in oven until cheese is golden, about 18 minutes.
  5. Cut pizza crosswise into slices.
