Edible Cookie Dough

Description

A chance to enjoy chocolate chip cookies without the need to wait for them to finish baking. Being made without egg means this recipe is safe to consume without the need for baking. With proper storage this cookie dough will remain good for up to 3 months.

Despite the exclusion of egg the consumption of raw flour remains within the recipe. It is therefore a requirement to heat-treat the flour in the recipe before adding it to the dough. Heat-treatment can be done by way of a microwave or oven, choose whichever option works best for you and enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Heat-treat your flour. Place flour in a microwave-safe bowl and cook for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring every 15 seconds. If using oven, place on baking sheet and toast at 350 degrees for 5 to 6 minutes
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add heat-treated flour, mix until a crumbly dough forms.
  3. Stir in milk until dough is just combined. Fold in both milk and mini chocolate chips
